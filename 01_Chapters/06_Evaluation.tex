\section{Introduction}

The evaluation of this prototype is based on the evaluation methods \textit{Testing} and \textit{Descriptive} as proposed by \cite{Hevner2004} and illustrated in Figure \ref{tbl:designevaluationmethods} in Chapter \ref{EvaluationMethodology}. The \textit{Testing} evaluation method that focus on the functional aspect in executing the artefact interfaces to find defects and failures as well as the structural aspect for metric evaluation has been part of the artefact development and therefore is already covered in Chapter \ref{ChapterDevelopment}. The \textit{Descriptive} evaluation methods are broken further down into \textit{Scenarios} and \textit{Informed Arguments} which are discussed in the following chapters.
